SPACE AND BEYOND

Space is endless and not without beauty. One just needs to look up at the nights sky and see the stars and planets. Here you will see some of most beautiful photos of planets and galaxies.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the sun. It is the only planet in our solar system that supports life.

Facts

  • Earth is 4.5 billion years old.
  • Earth has a population of 8 billion people.
  • Earth is 71% water.

Mars

Mars is the 4th planet from the sun. It is called the "Red Planet". It is often referred to in movies as the future home of mankind. There are currently small robotics with cameras photographing and returning data to Earth.

Facts

  • Mars has two moons .
  • Mars is the 2nd smallest planet.
  • Mars was named after the Roman god of war.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the 5th planet from the sun. It is the largest planet in our solar system.

Milky Way Galaxy

This galaxy is the home of our solar system which is the only known source of life, for now.

Facts

  • The Milky Way was born about 12.5 billion years ago.
  • The Ancient Greeks also looked upon the Milky Way as a road that led to Mount Olympus.
  • The Milky Way moves through space at a velocity of about 552 kilometres per second (343 miles per second) with respect to the Cosmic Microwave Background radiation.
